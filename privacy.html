Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2024

1. Privacy-First Design

StockGenie is built with a privacy-first philosophy. We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share your name to use the application. Your stock watchlists and app preferences are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

2. Subscription Diagnostics (RevenueCat)

To manage premium subscriptions and ensure you have access to the features you've purchased, we use a service called RevenueCat. When you interact with our subscription features, RevenueCat may collect technical identifiers (such as your App Store anonymous ID) and transaction history. This data is used strictly for diagnostic purposes, such as verifying your purchase status, processing restores, and troubleshooting subscription issues. We do not use this data to track your identity outside of the app.

3. Market Data Sources

Our AI analysis relies on market data from providers like Polygon.io and Finnhub. These services receive requests for stock data but do not receive any information about you or your specific usage of the app. We do not share any user-specific data with these providers.

4. Information We Do Not Collect

We are committed to your privacy and do not collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII). This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number.
  • Your precise location data.
  • Your financial information or brokerage account details.

5. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the minimal technical information we process. However, no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at StockGenie@fromamerica-llc.com.
